Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring that the Old World Wisconsin Project website is accessible to everyone. Our site adheres to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) to provide an inclusive experience for all users.

We continually strive to improve our accessibility practices and welcome feedback on how we can enhance the user experience.
